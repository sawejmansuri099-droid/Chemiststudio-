Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work? +

Aapki reception patient ka WhatsApp number dashboard mein add karti hai. Chemist Studio automatically ek review request bhej deta hai — patient tap karta hai aur Google Review page seedha khul jaata hai.

Can my receptionist use it? +

Bilkul. Dashboard itna simple hai ki koi bhi team member 5 minute mein seekh sakta hai — koi technical background zaroori nahi.

Does the patient need another app? +

Nahi. Sab kuch WhatsApp par hota hai, jo patient already use karta hai. Koi extra download ya signup nahi chahiye.

How long does setup take? +

Most clinics ek hi din mein live ho jaate hain. Hamari team aapke Google Business profile ko connect karke poora setup kar deti hai.

Is training included? +

Haan, onboarding ke saath free training session included hai — aapki poori reception team ke liye.

Is patient data secure? +

Bilkul. Patient data encrypted rehta hai aur sirf review request ke purpose se use hota hai — kabhi bhi third parties ke saath share nahi kiya jaata.
